The World Cup 2022: A Tournament to Remember

In November 2022, the world watched one of the most exciting football tournaments in history. For the first time, the FIFA World Cup took place in Qatar, a small country in the Middle East. This was also the first World Cup to happen in November and December, because summers in Qatar are extremely hot. Thirty-two national teams competed in the tournament. Each team played hard to reach the final and win the golden trophy. Some big teams, like Germany and Belgium, were eliminated early and went home before the knockout rounds. Morocco had an incredible tournament. The team defeated big nations like Spain and Portugal. They became the first African team to reach the semi-finals, and their fans celebrated loudly in stadiums around the world. The final match was between Argentina and France. It was one of the greatest finals ever. France's Kylian Mbappé scored a hat-trick – three goals in one game. However, Argentina's captain, Lionel Messi, led his team with amazing skill and determination. After 90 minutes, the score was 2-2. After extra time, the score was still 3-3, so the teams played a penalty shootout. Argentina won the penalty shootout and became World Cup champions. Millions of people in Argentina poured into the streets and cheered all night. For Messi, it was a very special moment. He finally won the one trophy he never had before. Many football fans agreed that he was now the greatest player of all time. The 2022 World Cup gave the world unforgettable moments, surprising results, and beautiful football. People are still talking about it today.

❓ Comprehension Questions
  • 1. Where did the 2022 FIFA World Cup take place?
  • 2. Why was the 2022 World Cup held in November and December instead of summer?
  • 3. Kylian Mbappé scored a hat-trick. What does "hat-trick" mean? Use information from the text to explain.
  • 4. Morocco was a surprising team in the tournament. What facts from the text show that their success was unexpected?
  • 5. Do you think Lionel Messi deserved to be called the greatest player of all time after the 2022 World Cup? Use at least one detail from the text to support your opinion.
